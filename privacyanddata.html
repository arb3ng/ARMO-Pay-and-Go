Privacy and Data Policy

At ARMO Boarding House website, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy and Data Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our online payment platform.

1. Information Collection

We may collect the following types of information when you use ARMO Pay and Go:

  • Personal information: Name, email address, contact number, and other relevant details.
  • Financial information: Payment details, billing address, and transaction history.

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To process and facilitate your rent and bill payments.
  • To communicate with you regarding payment confirmations, receipts, and account-related updates.
  • To improve our services, user experience, and customer support.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect against fraudulent activities.

3. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to outside parties unless necessary for providing our services or as required by law. We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our platform, conducting business, and serving you better.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy and Data Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

6. User Consent

By using ARMO Pay and Go, you consent to the collection, use, and storage of your personal information as described in this Privacy and Data Policy.

7. Updates to the Policy

We may update this Privacy and Data Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Privacy and Data Policy, please contact us through the ARMO Boarding House website.
